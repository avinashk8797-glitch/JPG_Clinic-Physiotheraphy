Our Services

Comprehensive Physiotherapy Services

We offer a wide range of specialized physiotherapy treatments to help you recover, heal, and live a pain-free life.

Aquatic Physiotherapy

Gentle water-based therapy that uses buoyancy to reduce joint stress, improve mobility, and support rehabilitation in a safe, low-impact environment.

Arthritis Treatment

Specialized techniques to reduce joint inflammation, manage chronic pain, improve flexibility, and maintain mobility for arthritis patients.

Back Pain Treatment

Targeted therapy to relieve back pain through therapeutic exercises, manual techniques, and posture correction for lasting relief and prevention.

Balance Exercise Therapy

Customized balance training programs to improve stability, prevent falls, and restore confidence in movement and daily activities.

Foot and Ankle Pain Treatment

Expert care for foot and ankle injuries, plantar fasciitis, sprains, and chronic pain using targeted exercises and manual therapy techniques.

Geriatric Physiotherapy

Gentle, age-appropriate physiotherapy to improve mobility, strength, and quality of life for elderly patients with compassionate, attentive care.

Heart Therapy Rehabilitation

Structured cardiac rehabilitation programs to strengthen the heart, improve endurance, and support recovery after heart-related conditions.

Hip Pain Treatment

Comprehensive hip pain management combining manual therapy, strengthening exercises, and mobility work for improved hip function and comfort.

Hydrotherapy Training

Therapeutic water exercises using warm water to relax muscles, reduce pain, improve blood circulation, and accelerate natural healing.

Knee Pain Treatment

Effective knee rehabilitation through targeted exercises, manual therapy, and progressive strengthening programs for pain-free movement.

Neurological Spine Physiotherapy

Specialized treatment for neurological spine conditions to improve nerve function, reduce pain, and restore healthy movement patterns.

Physical Therapy

Comprehensive physical therapy programs designed to improve strength, flexibility, and overall physical function through evidence-based techniques.

Post-Surgical Rehabilitation

Expert guided recovery programs after surgery to restore function, rebuild strength, and support safe return to daily activities.

Shoulder Pain Treatment

Targeted shoulder rehabilitation to treat frozen shoulder, rotator cuff injuries, and chronic pain with personalized therapy plans.

Spinal Injuries Treatment

Advanced spinal injury rehabilitation to improve mobility, manage pain, and maximize recovery potential with specialized techniques.

Therapeutic Exercise Programs

Customized exercise programs designed to treat specific conditions, improve physical fitness, and promote long-term wellness and recovery.

Vestibular Rehabilitation Therapy

Specialized therapy for dizziness, vertigo, and balance disorders through targeted vestibular exercises and neuromuscular retraining.
